This study lab combines the project architecture guide with an adaptive quiz, flashcards, and locally stored mastery tracking.

## Why it exists

The larger music-credit graph project spans data engineering, graph storage, Python services, container orchestration, networking, and public delivery. The study lab turns those choices into something reviewable before and during implementation.

## What is included

- The complete architecture and technology study guide
- Adaptive questions that repeat weak areas
- Flashcard and quick-review modes
- Architecture scenarios rather than pure terminology recall
- Browser-local progress with no account or backend

## Current state

The application is a self-contained HTML artifact. Astro and Pages CMS manage this catalog entry and its project notes, while the experiment remains portable and independently runnable.
